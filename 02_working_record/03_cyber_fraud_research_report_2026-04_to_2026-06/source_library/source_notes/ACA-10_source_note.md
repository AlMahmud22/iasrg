## Information Used
This source supports the report's discussion that phishing mitigation requires both technical and human-centered approaches. It is used as a legal open-access replacement for the blocked ScienceDirect anti-phishing training review and supports the design of scenario practice, cue detection, verification behavior, and the need to avoid relying only on awareness messages.

## Report Placement
- Chapters supported: 2, 4, 5
- Seven-factor mapping: F1, F4, F7

## Caveats And Limits
This article is about phishing mitigation broadly and is not Malaysia-specific. It should be used to support intervention design and training limitations, not national prevalence claims.

## Implementation Note
Use this source for open-access evidence on anti-phishing mitigation categories, human-centered mitigation needs, and the limits of technology-only responses.
