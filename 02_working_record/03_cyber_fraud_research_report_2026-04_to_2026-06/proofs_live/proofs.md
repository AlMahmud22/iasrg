## Capture Quality Checklist

- Capture only live pages or official PDFs that are accessible at the time of capture.
- Keep enough page context visible to identify the source.
- Do not capture login pages, popups, personal data, or user-entered form fields.
- Do not edit the screenshot in a way that changes evidence meaning.
- Use proof screenshots as supporting visuals only; keep full source links in the references.
- If a source cannot be captured cleanly, document the reason instead of replacing it with an AI-generated image.
